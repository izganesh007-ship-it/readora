# Built-In Legal / Info Pages

The preview includes ready-to-edit content for:

- About
- Contact
- Privacy Policy
- Terms of Service

The wording matches Readora's actual logic:

- free books are read-only unless download is explicitly enabled
- paid books require purchase
- paid ebook links are one-time only
- links expire after first redemption or after 24 hours
- payment verification uses BTCPay Server
- ebook files are private and delivered through signed URLs
- audit logs are kept for security and failed download support

Before launch, replace placeholder contact email `support@readora.example` with your real email.
